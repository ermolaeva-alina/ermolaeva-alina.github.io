My approach

User centered design

I actively learn best practices, research, test, and iteratively refine the design based on feedback to ensure the final product meets user needs.

Empathy

Empathy allows me to deeply understand the needs, fears, and pain of users, allowing me to create solutions that address not only the functional requirements, but also the emotional aspects of their experience.

Helicopter view

I employ a 'helicopter view' to assess the broader context and project goals, guiding design decisions aligned with strategic objectives. This ensures a cohesive and forward-thinking user interface, contributing to overall project success.

Systems thinking

I use systems thinking to create flexible design systems. By analyzing the relationships and elements within a system, I create holistic and scalable solutions, ensuring consistency across all design projects.
